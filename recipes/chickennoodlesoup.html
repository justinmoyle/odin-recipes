Homemade Chicken Noodle Soup

Home

Description

This homemade chicken noodle soup is soul-warming and deliciously simple â€” just chicken and noodles. What makes it so good is the homemade roasted chicken stock.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Carrot
  • Diced Onion
  • Celery
  • Thyme Leaves
  • Salt
  • Chicken Fat
  • Roasted Chicken Broth
  • Wide Egg Noodles

Steps

  1. Step 1:Melt butter in a large soup pot over medium heat. Stir in carrot, onion, celery, thyme, and salt. Add chicken fat; cook and stir until onions turn soft and translucent, 5 to 6 minutes.
  2. Step 2:Stir in roasted chicken broth and bring to a boil. Season with salt, if necessary.
  3. Step 3:Stir in egg noodles; cook until tender, about 5 minutes.
  4. Step 4:Add cooked chicken breast meat; simmer until heated through, about 5 minutes. Season with cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper.
  5. Step 5:Serve hot and enjoy!
